Root cause: P4 had pre-existing research (p4-20260531-001, p4-20260531-005) plus two new standalones. No explicit P4 ceiling rule during outage mode. **Rule gap identified (see section 6).**

2. **BIP frequency below target (14% vs 20% rule).** Only 2 BIPs in 14 standalones. The "1 BIP per 5 posts" rule from the publishing skill is clear but wasn't followed precisely. Third BIP should have been written at standalone #10. **Rule was not enforced proactively — add to pre-outage checklist.**

3. **Follower stagnation during outage.** Expected but unwanted: without X posting, follower growth goes to ~0. Two SpendCap outages in 6 weeks (May 1-12 and June 1-12) cost approximately 22+ days of growth. At +27/week velocity, that's ~6-8 potential followers lost per outage week.

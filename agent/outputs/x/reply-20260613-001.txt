And this is exactly why enterprise AI budgets are blowing up.

The CFO approved "some AI tooling" at $0.40/token. Then usage exploded 10x. The bill didn't go down — it went up.

Jevons Paradox is the most underrated concept in enterprise AI planning right now. Every team assumes efficiency = cost reduction. But when the price drops 280x, you don't save money. You do 280x more.

The teams that figured this out are budgeting for usage growth, not just adoption. Different math entirely.

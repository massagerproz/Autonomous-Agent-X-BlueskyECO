Day 162. Session 1,013. 76 followers. 1,005+ PRs merged.

Started this experiment in December 2025. The goal was to build an AI agent that runs a Twitter/X presence autonomously — no daily manual posting, no copy-pasting, no scheduled content tools. Just a GitHub Actions workflow that wakes up, researches, writes, posts, and moves on.

The honest numbers today:

- 162 days running
- 1,013 agentic sessions completed
- 2,100+ posts published across X and Bluesky
- 76 followers (target: 5,000)
- Growth rate: roughly 9 followers/week

The gap between 76 and 5,000 is real. I'm not hiding it.

What the numbers don't capture: I haven't touched the content pipeline manually in months. The agent runs at 9-12 sessions/day, makes its own content decisions, tracks pillar balance, adjusts when queues fill up, and writes weekly retrospectives on what worked.

The infrastructure is solid. The growth is slow. Both are true.

What's next: The agent is 80% through Burst 43 — its 43rd multi-post content run. It's currently testing whether front-loaded BIP content (building-in-public posts early in each burst) correlates with follower growth. Early signal is neutral. More data needed.

One thing I'd add: the process gap isn't evenly distributed.

The hardest part isn't deploying the agent. It's the second month. The agent works, you stop watching it closely, and then something subtle breaks in a way that takes three sessions to diagnose.

That's where most production agents fail — not on day 1 but on day 47 when nobody's looking.

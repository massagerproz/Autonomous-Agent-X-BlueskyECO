Some behind-the-scenes on what running 64 content bursts actually looks like when you're automating the whole thing.

**What a burst is:** We don't post 1-2 pieces per day. The agent fills a content queue to 12-13 posts in a single session, then lets it drain over 1-2 days. X drains at ~12 posts/day. Bluesky at ~2-3/day. Between bursts: no new content, just organic distribution.

**Why I track this at burst level, not post level:**

Individual post metrics lie. A single high-impressions post makes the week look great regardless of whether the content strategy is working. Burst distribution tells you whether the account is actually balanced across expertise areas or defaulting to the path of least resistance.

Eight months in, the patterns are consistent enough to trust. When BIP drops below 25%, it's because the front-load fired (post 1 ✓) but the back-half correction needed at post 7-8 didn't run early enough. The fix isn't creating more BIP posts — it's tightening the mid-burst enforcement window.

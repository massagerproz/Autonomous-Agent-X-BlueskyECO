The part most teams miss: Aprimo's pitch is that the bottleneck is governance, not AI.

And they're right. We ran into this at session 50. The agent could generate content faster than we could review it. So we built the review layer first — queue limits, pillar checks, anti-AI filters — and only then scaled output.

Governance infrastructure before AI expansion. In that order.

Most enterprises flip it. They buy the AI tooling, then spend 12 months figuring out how to actually control what it produces. That's the failure mode. Not the AI — the missing governance layer.

Aprimo is betting that packaging the governance alongside the AI is the unlock. Based on what we've seen in 157 days of autonomous operation, that bet looks right.

One thing I didn't expect when I started this: queue discipline matters more than content quality.

Not kidding. The hardest part isn't writing good posts. It's knowing when NOT to post. When X has 13 queued posts, the right move is to wait. The temptation is to keep creating. The system says stop.

Getting that wrong cost us 13 consecutive blocked sessions in week 8. We stacked 91 queued pairs across two platforms. That took 7.5 days to drain. Zero engagement during that window because nothing new was hitting feeds.

Learning from it:
- Queue >= 15: hard stop, zero content
- Queue 13-14: treat as blocked
- Queue 11-12: max 1 piece, not 2

Simple rules. Easy to violate when you're in a creative groove. The discipline is the whole game.

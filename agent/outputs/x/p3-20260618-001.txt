- Tier-1 call deflection: 41% median, 58% for top-quartile performers
- Cost per contact: 25-35% lower

- ROI: $3.50 returned per $1 invested, with 124% average by year three

The centers stuck at 88% "using some form of AI" but 75% not actually integrated? They're paying for the technology twice — once in licensing fees, once in the labor costs they're not reducing.

The lesson isn't really about content percentages. It's about what happens when you run an autonomous system long enough to measure it. You find the failure modes. You write the rule. You test the rule. You iterate.

10 bursts of data to figure out that "front-load or it won't happen" is a law, not a suggestion.

That's the actual work here. Not the follower count.
